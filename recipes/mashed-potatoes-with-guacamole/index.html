Mashed Potatoes with Guacamole

by Rob

1 serving

  • 2 medium potatoes
  • 1 medium avocado
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Tajin seasoning
  1. Put the potatoes in a pot of water
  2. Gradually boil for 15 minutes
  3. Strain the water from the pot
  4. With the heat on, start mashing
  5. Stir in butter and spices, gradually adding milk
  6. Finish mashing the potatoes
  7. In a bowl, slice then mash the avocado flesh
  8. Plate the potatoes, guacamole on top
  9. Season the top with tajin
