Banana Bread

by Rob

1 loaf

  • 4 overripe bananas
  • 2/3 stick of butter
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate chips
  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. In a bowl, mash bananas until smooth
  3. Melt the butter and mix it in
  4. Add the sugar, egg, baking soda, & flour
  5. Add the chocolate chips
  6. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan
  7. Top with additional chocolate chips
  8. Bake for 60 minutes
  9. Loosen with a rubber spatula
