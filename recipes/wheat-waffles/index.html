Wheat Waffles

by Rob

4 waffles

  • 1 cup of whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 Tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 2 Tbsp of brown sugar
  • 3 shakes of cinnamon
  1. Start the waffle iron at medium-low heat
  2. Mix the ingredients in a big bowl
  3. Pour with a ladle
