Chocolate Chip Cookies

by Rob

12 cookies

  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 3 cups wheat flour
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 sticks of butter
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F
  2. Melt the butter in a pot
  3. Mix it all in no particular order
  4. Make dough balls on unoiled trays
  5. Bake for 12 minutes
Dessert Sweet
